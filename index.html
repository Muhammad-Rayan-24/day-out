Crucial Timing Note

Because arcades in Lahore are mostly located inside major malls, they do not open until 11:00 AM or 12:00 PM. For your 9 AM start, we recommend beginning with a romantic breakfast/brunch or a park stroll, then heading to the arcade around 11:30 AM.

If You Change Your Mind...

Arcades are loud. If you decide you want something a bit more serene or culturally enriching between 9 AM and 4 PM, here are top alternatives.

🏛️

Lahore Fort & Badshahi Mosque

Perfect for a 9 AM start before the sun gets too hot. Incredible architecture, great for taking beautiful photos of her.

Budget: Low (Entry fees ~Rs. 100)
🦁

Lahore Safari Zoo Drive

A bit further out (Raiwind Road), but doing the drive-through safari in the morning is a very unique date experience.

Budget: Medium (Rs. 2000+)
🎬

Morning Matinee (Cinepax/Cue)

Catch a 12 PM movie show. It's cool, quiet, and gives you plenty to talk about at lunch afterwards.

Budget: Medium (Rs. 3000 for two)
